EdgeQEC v12.0 — Full Integrated Codebase

v12 Improvements over v11

Differentiable Decoder — Soft/differentiable MWPM and Correlated paths via Sinkhorn relaxation and learned edge reweighting, enabling end-to-end gradient flow through the full hierarchy

Learned Tanner-Aware Message Passing — Edge features (stabiliser type, weight, round index) added to TannerGNNEncoder; full heterogeneous message passing replaces uniform GAT

Multi-Qubit Logical Observables — Full distance-d logical error rate tracking across all k logical qubits; LogicalObservableTracker replaces single-qubit LER

Real-Device Closed Loop — QiskitBackend and QiboBackend stubs wired to dynamic circuit feedback; syndrome extraction circuits submitted and results fed back in real time

Energy + Thermal Feedback Loop — CurriculumManager coupled to real power/thermal sensor model; stage advancement now gated on measured power budget

Scaling to qLDPC / Hypergraph-Product Codes — TannerGNNEncoder extended to handle hypergraph-product code structure; qLDPCCode fully wired into all training paths
